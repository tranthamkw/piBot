Function and Purpose

I am a simple platform upon which electrical, mechanical, and software engineering can be taught and explored.  The key concepts which may be explored are

 

Electrical engineering:

  • Battery charging management,
  • RS485 serial bus communication,
  • Pulse-width-modulation motor speed control,
  • Servo motor position control, and
  • Digital display devices.

Mechanical engineering:

  • strength of materials,
  • robot locomotion and steering strategies

Software engineering:

  • embedded microprocessor control systems ( Embedded Systems),
  • stereoscopic computer vision,
  • Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) ( SLAM ),
  • HTML and PHP coding.

 

 

Hardware design

The central computer is a Raspberry Pi Model XX running Debian/Raspbian operating system. The computer connects to a variety of peripheral devices through a proprietary RS485 serial bus, which allow the robot to interact with the physical world.  The peripheral devices are called “guests” and the Raspberry Pi is the “Host”.  Communication is managed through an addressable serial bus.

Peripheral Interface Boards

 

Raspi Interface Board V4.

Provides electrical I/O for RS-485 bus, and control two CFAF128 display devices. This attaches directly to the Raspberry Pi computer through the GPIO connection. Schematic.

Ultrasonic Ranger and servo control

This device is used to measure distances to obstacles. It works by sending out a burst of ultra-sonic sound and listening for an echo.  This device also permits aiming the distance sensor with a servomotor.
